Privacy Policy – OK to Wake Clock

Last updated: February 2025

OK to Wake Clock ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy. This policy describes what data the app uses and where it is stored.

Data collection

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with us or any third party. It does not use analytics, advertising, or account sign-in.

Data stored on your device

The app stores your settings only on your device (e.g. wake time, brightness, pre-wake duration). This data stays on your phone and is not sent anywhere. If you uninstall the app, this data is removed.

Permissions

The app may use:

  • Keep screen on – so the clock stays visible.
  • Device administrator (optional) – only if you enable "Lock screen when leaving app"; used solely to lock the device when you leave the app.

No other data is accessed or shared.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the app’s GitHub repository or contact the developer through the Play Store listing.
